Joseph P.C.
Capturing Stories, One Frame at a Time

Joseph P.C. is a passionate photographer from a small town in the district of Kannur, Kerala. His love for travel and photography has taken him on numerous journeys, capturing emotions, street life, and breathtaking landscapes.

Driven by this deep interest, he pursued Communication, Media, and Psychology, further enhancing his ability to tell stories through his lens. Currently, he is pursuing his Bachelor's degree at Christ (Deemed to be University), Bangalore.

Joseph started Pixel Stories to share his unique perspective of the world. With a keen eye for detail and a heart for exploration, he continues to document the world around him, turning fleeting moments into timeless memories.
