STREET PHOTOGRAPHY

Street photography is all about observing the world and capturing fleeting moments as they happen. It’s unposed, unscripted, and full of everyday life. A bustling market, a quiet alley, or a stranger’s fleeting smile—each frame tells a small, honest story.

Unlike studio photography, where everything is carefully arranged, street photography thrives on spontaneity. It requires patience, quick reflexes, and a keen eye for interesting details. Some shots are about contrast—light and shadow playing on city walls—while others focus on emotion, like a shared laugh or a moment of solitude in a crowded place.
