NATURE PHOTOGRAPHY

Nature photography is the art of capturing the beauty, essence, and intricacies of the natural world through a camera lens. It focuses on landscapes, wildlife, plants, and natural phenomena, aiming to preserve moments that showcase the Earth’s raw and untouched splendor.

Whether it’s the dramatic sweep of a mountain range, the delicate details of a flower, or the fleeting behavior of animals in their habitats, nature photography is about telling the story of the planet’s diversity and wonder.
